Otacon's Three Favorite Anime of All Time

Steins;Gate

Based on a series of visual novels, this anime is a scientist's (like myself) dream... or maybe nightmare. It follows a self-proclaimed mad-scientist and his quest to conquer time itself. He gets his time machine to work, but at what cost?

Clannad and Clannad:After Story

Also based on a set of visual novels, Clannad starts like plenty of anime do, following high schoolers around. This anime seeks to break the high school romance cliche by showing what happens to the high school sweethearts after high school ends. What follows is a tale of love, family, and lost. Otacon cried more than he ever has before watching the last 10 episodes of this series, so viewer beware!

Bakemonogatari

Created by the legendary Japanese novelist, Nisio Isin, the Monogatari series follows Koyomi Araragi as he encounters various abberations, or monsters, throughout his journeys in his small, sleepy hometown. A homesick crab inhabits his girlfriend, a local basketball star grows a monkey's paw, and Koyomi himself is bitten by a vampire! It is animated by the prestigious Shaft Studio who brings their avant-garde style to the storyboard to give this anime a real unique feel. There are many books and series in the monogatari series so the fun will never stop!
