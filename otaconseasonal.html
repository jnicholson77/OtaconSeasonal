Otacon's Seasonal Picks (Winter 2019 Season)

Dororo

This samurai anime tells the story of Hyakkimaru and Dororo as they embark on a journey to get back Hyakkimaru's stolen body parts. Yes, you heard that right. Hyakkimaru's father was a feudal lord who made a deal with the devil to bring prosperity to his land, but in return he had to sacrifice his first born son's body parts to the demons. Hyakkimaru and Dororo must fight these demons to reclaim Hyakkimaru's senses and body parts and get revenge on his father. It's a wild, dramatic ride!

Mob Psycho 100 II

This series's main character is a middle school student named Mob who just so happens to be an esper. The anime follows him and his master, Reigen, who just so happens to not have esper powers. Still, Mob is a caring kid and helps Reigen out with his business, Spirits Consultation and Such. Oh I almost forgot, if Mob gets too emotional and gets to 100% anger, he explodes.

Kaguya-sama: Love is War

This anime takes place in, you guessed it, a good ol' prestigious Japanese high school. This series centers around the student council president and the student council vice president and their romantic hijinks. They are in love with each other, but neither wants to admit it to the other because in their minds admitting you love someone is defeat and love is war. Tom-foolery naturally ensues.

The Promised Neverland

This anime takes place at Grace Field House, an orphange for children. The children are well-fed, clean, and have everything they could ever need. They are taken care of by a woman named Isabella who they refer to as "Momma". There are only a few rules: never go to the gate leading to the outside world and when a child turns 12 years old they are adopted into a family and leave the orphanage. But what if the orphanage was really just a farm that harvested children as food for demons? No, Otacon's imagination is not getting away from him. That is literally the plot of this horrific anime.

The Rising of the Shield Hero

Young student Naofumi is sucked into a library book and transported to a different world where he is one of 4 heroes that are tasked with saving the people of this world: the sword hero, the spear hero, the bow hero, and the shield hero. He just so happens to become the shield hero who everyone despises because what can a man with just a shield really do? Turns out, not a whole lot besides piss everyone off.
